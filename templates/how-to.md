# How-To Article Template

## Structure
- Clear title starting with "How to..." or "A Guide to..."
- Intro paragraph explaining what the reader will learn and why it matters
- Step-by-step sections:
  - ## Step N: Action Name
  - Clear instructions
  - Tips or warnings where relevant
- Summary / what to do next

## Tone
- Helpful and authoritative
- Assume the reader is a beginner unless the topic is advanced
- Use "you" and direct instructions

## Length
- 600-1000 words
- 3-8 steps depending on complexity

## SEO
- Title includes the primary action keyword
- Description promises a clear outcome
- Tags should include the skill area and related topics
