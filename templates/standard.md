# Standard Article Template

## Structure
- Informative title that includes the primary keyword
- Intro paragraph (2-3 sentences) establishing the topic and why it matters now
- 3-5 content sections with ## headings
- Each section: 2-4 paragraphs with specific details, data, or examples
- Conclusion paragraph with key takeaways

## Tone
- Informative and engaging
- Support claims with specifics — names, dates, numbers
- Reference current events or trends where relevant

## Length
- 600-1000 words

## SEO
- Title is clear and keyword-rich
- Description summarizes the key insight
- Tags cover the topic area and related subjects
