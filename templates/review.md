# Review Article Template

## Structure
- Title: "[Subject] Review: [Key Verdict]"
- Intro paragraph with quick verdict (1-2 sentences)
- Overview section: what is the subject, who is it for
- Pros section (## What We Like)
- Cons section (## What Could Be Better)
- Detailed analysis sections (2-3 topic areas)
- Verdict / recommendation paragraph
- Rating or recommendation level

## Tone
- Honest and balanced
- Take a clear position — don't be wishy-washy
- Use specific examples and comparisons

## Length
- 800-1500 words

## SEO
- Title includes the product/subject name and "review"
- Description includes the verdict
- Tags should include the product category and brand
